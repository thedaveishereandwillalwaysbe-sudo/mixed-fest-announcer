Completely stuck? The 5 things that always work

  1. Announcer silent? Click ● Go on air once. That unlocks sound.
  2. Announcer out of sync? ⏸ Pause, then +30s / −30s, or ⏭ End now.
  3. Scores not in Tournify? They never auto-arrive — type them in by hand at the live page.
  4. No internet? Run off the paper schedule + paper results, enter into Tournify later.
  5. Total meltdown? Whistle + paper schedule + keep matches moving. Phone Hunt or Babs.
