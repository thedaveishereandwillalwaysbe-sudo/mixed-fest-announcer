House Rules

Games & Rules - 7-a-Side Hockey

Team Composition

  • 3 male & 3 female outfield players
  • 1 goalkeeper/kicking back (either sex)
  • Agree exceptions with opponent captain

Match Rules

  • 20 minutes one way
  • 5 minute break between matches
  • Centrally timed
  • Foghorn signals start & end

Playing Regulations

  • Short Corners: Female player first strike
  • Reverse Stick Strikes: STRICTLY DISALLOWED
  • Tomahawks: STRICTLY DISALLOWED
  • Mouthguards: Required (England Hockey)

Umpiring & Results

  • Each team provides umpire for own matches
  • Update Tournify results immediately
  • No umpire = point deduction risk

Scoring System

  • 3 points for a win
  • 1 point for a draw
  • Tied teams: penalty flick competition

Absence & Disputes

  • Miss match = opponent wins 3-0
  • Festival Committee decision is FINAL
  • Play fairly & sportsmanlike

Team Agreement & Liability

⚠️ Important - Please Read

Participants in the Festival should be aware that while the Club carries insurance for the event this provides cover only for public liability. It does not cover individuals or teams for any loss or damage that they may suffer as a direct result of their participation in the Festival which is entirely at their own risk and responsibility. The Festival Committee accepts no responsibility or liability for any such loss or damage.

The teams and each individual agree that:

  1. They will accept and act upon any instructions given by the Organisers, umpires and/or club officials.
  2. Games will be played in a sportsmanlike manner.
  3. Anyone drunk or acting aggressively may be asked to leave a game.
